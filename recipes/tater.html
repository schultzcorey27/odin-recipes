Tater Tot Casserole

Description

This is a tater tot casserole.


Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 can (10.5 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 16 oz frozen tater tots
  • 2 cups Cheddar cheese, shredded
  • salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F or 175 degrees C
  2. Cook ground beef over medium high heat and stir until meat is brown and crumbling, about 7-10 minutes
  3. Stir in cream of mushroom soup and add salt and pepper to taste
  4. Move beef mixture to a 9" by 13" baking dish
  5. Layer tater tots over the beef mixture, then spread cheese on top of them
  6. Bake until the tater tots are golden brown, about 30 to 45 minutes
  7. Enjoy the experience of having a hamburger and tater tots without having to gather any condiments!
