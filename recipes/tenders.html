BBQ Chicken Tenders

Description

Do you enjoy chicken tenders? Are you getting tired of eating them breaded with mustard, soy sauce, or whatever else you have in your fridge?
Well, good news, because I have the solution: BBQ chicken tenders! All the sweet flavors of the grill without the need to own one yourself!


Ingredients

  • vegetable oil cooking spray
  • 1/3 cup BBQ sauce of your choice
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Asian BBQ seasoning
  • 1 tbsp caramel sauce
  • 1 lb chicken tenders

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F or 190 degrees C
  2. Stir the BBQ sauce, BBQ seasoning, and caramel sauce in a small bowl until thoroughly combined. Set aside 2 tbsp of the mixture for basting later
  3. Coat each chicken tender in the sauce by dipping it, then place them into a casserole dish
  4. Bake for about 25 minutes
  5. Baste with remaining 2 tbsp of sauce and bake for an additional 5 minutes
  6. Picture yourself on a sunny July day, eating these in the backyard with all the homies, and enjoy
