Buttered Noodles

Description

This dish is a staple among those who appreciate fine Italian cuisine but balk at the idea of adding a sauce or vegetables to the dish.
Enjoy this classic in comfort food with some Parmesan cheese for added deliciousness!


Ingredients

  • 16 oz fettuccine noodles
  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil. Salt to taste
  2. Add fettuccine to pot and return to a boil
  3. Cook over medium heat until tender (8-10 minutes), stirring occasionally
  4. Drain pot of water, then add butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Stir until combined
  5. Enjoy your buttered noodles!
